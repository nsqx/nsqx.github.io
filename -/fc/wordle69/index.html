How to play


In Wordle 69, you have 6 (standard) attempts at trying to guess a 5-letter word. If the letter does not exist in the word, it will not be highlighted. If it does appear in the word, but is in the incorrect spot, it will be highlighted in yellow. If it does appear in the word and is in the correct spot, it will be highlighted in green. Using these hints, guess the word! Have fun!— nsq.
